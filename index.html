How to Play Quoridor

🎯 Objective:

• Player 1 (Red) must reach the bottom row

• Player 2 (Green) must reach the top row

🚶 Movement:

• Move one square in any direction (up, down, left, right)

• Jump over opponent if they're adjacent

• Make diagonal jumps when straight jump is blocked

🧱 Walls:

• Each player starts with 10 walls

• Walls block movement between squares

• Cannot completely block opponent's path to goal

• Walls span 2 squares and can be horizontal or vertical

🎮 Controls:

• Click "Move" then click a highlighted square

• Click "Wall" then click a wall preview to place it
